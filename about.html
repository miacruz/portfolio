I’m Mia Cruz, and I am currently a third-year Design major and Computer Science and Philippine Studies minor at the University of San Francisco. I also work for USF’s Web Services as a Web Assistant. I’m originally from the East Bay and I am a first-generation Filipino-American.

The intersection of design and technology has always been something that intrigued me growing up, leading up to my passion and interest in UI/UX design. My goal is to further my skills in both graphic and web design and be able to combine those to create a more accessible and inclusive space in the digital and art world.

Outside of design, you can find me exploring the city, spending quality time with my loved ones, baking, reading, or listening to music.
